Community Report

For Vancouver Heritage Foundation

For the community report, I used an imaginative story to convey foundation’s ideas and goals. The layout is simple and clean, featuring illustrations inspired by children's books. .

The story includes a tree representing dying history and a bird symbolizing the foundation. The colour palette is based on the foundation's heritage colours dark red and beige with added greens for contrast. I used a simple serif font alongside a handwritten style font for titles to create a fairytale atmosphere.
